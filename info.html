All about Note.ly

Our mission at Note.ly is to help you manage your information more easily.

Notes, reminders, lists, links, images. Note.ly helps you keep track of all of these visually on asingle online canvas instead of notes stuck to your computer, desk or fridge.

Note.ly's perfect for individuals looking to access their notes anywhere or teams looking to share ideas and collaborate. Best yet, it’s amazingly simple. You’ll be creating notes in seconds.
